| Professional Biography |

Andrew Burns is a Website Delivery Trainer for FindLaw, a company of Thomson Reuters, and a former Full Stack Developer student at the University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp.He completed the course to learn a new valuable skill that will be useful later with his future career endeavors.During his personal time, Andrew enjoys spending time with his wife, spending time outdoors, playing music, and hanging out with friends and family

Before joining the University of Minnesota Bootcamp, Andrew spent his first five years of his career in the classroom. He taught social studies classes ranging from U.S History to Global Studies for students ranging from fourth grade to twelfth grade. Having his extensive education background, he hopes that he will be able to create applications and resources that can be used in the classrooms of tomorrow by learning how to program.
